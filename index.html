type of cryptocurrency exchange which allows for direct peer-to-peer cryptocurrency transactions to take place online securely and without the need for an intermediary. In transactions made through decentralized exchanges, the typical third party entities which would normally oversee the security and transfer of assets (e.g. banks, stockbrokers, online payment gateways, government institutions, etc.) are substituted by a blockchain or distributed ledger.

NFT Marketplace

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Tokens which means that tokens cannot be regenerated or replaced. NFT is a crypto technology such as a digital certificate that certifies the value and identity of the owner of the work, either photos, videos, or other virtual forms.

Staking

Crypto staking is an activity where a user of crypto assets can gain money simply by validating transactions or any activities that occur on the blockchain system. However, users must first store or lock their crypto assets in a digital wallet so that they are considered "worthy" and "legitimate" to get cash.
